Amber Rose Praises Her New Man After Dating Narcissists, Cheaters, And Abusers

 Posted on January 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  415 words  |  Martina Birk

Amber Rose has never been shy when it comes to her past and the men she dated. More than once, she said on social media that she’d been hurt a lot of narcissistic, abusive people.

Now, thankfully things are quite different with her new boyfriend, and she proclaims her love for him in public.

‘He thinks he soooo cute! 🙄😒 He really is tho 😩🤗😍 and sweet, smart, talented, charismatic and overall just an amazing man. In the past I’ve used the words “best friend” very loosely while speaking of my ex’s but I can honestly say I feel like this man really is my BEST FRIEND! We talk for hours, he makes me a better person, he listens to me and understands me. He also came into my life at a time where I’ve had enough of the narcissistic, cheating and abuse I’ve endured over the years from my previous relationships…..’ Amber wrote.

So @ae4president Thank you for all of ur love and support baby. I love you so much. 😘🤗🥰😇’

Her followers appreciated what she has at the moment, but some people also slammed her for changing men to quickly.

Someone said ‘He’s your man until you find another one, you don’t even know the definition of a good man because you ain’t had no man besides dating KIDS.. I don’t see any change you’re still the same 🤷🏿‍♀️💯’
