Natalie Zea Marries Travis Schuldt: The Following Star's Wedding

 Posted on March 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  282 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Claire and Keith are hitched!

The Following‘s Natalie Zea and Scrubs alum Travis Schuldt have tied the knot, a rep for the actress confirms exclusively to Us Weekly. The couple said “I do” in Hawaii on Wednesday, July 16.

Zea and Schuldt, both 39, married at a private residence in front of 18 family members. The blushing bride stunned in a long-sleeve white lace gown by Israeli designer Inbal Dror and wore her tousled light locks down, cascading past her shoulders. The groom, for his part, wore a Theory suit with a white shirt.

After the ceremony, the (barefoot!) newlyweds treated their guests to an intimate luau on the beach. Everyone feasted on Hawaiian favorites such as Lomi Lomi salmon, sautéed mahi mahi with macadamia nut butter, taro and sweet potato salad, tropical fruit salad and imu style roasted Kalua pork. If room, guests then noshed on a guava cake with buttercream icing for dessert.

Last month, Zea joked about her upcoming nuptials—and her lack of memory—via Twitter on June 28. “I just realized my wedding dress has been in my trunk for 4 days and I totally forgot about it. #WhereIsMyBrideGene?” she wrote. On July 9, she added: “My fiancé & I have a new favorite game: let’s see who can make the other one have the most second thoughts. #ImWinning #Marriage.”

Zea and Schuldt first met while working on the soap opera Passions, and have been together for 11 years. Zea appeared on the FOX’s drama The Following, and previously played Winona Hawkins on Justified from 2010 to 2014. Schuldt appeared in It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia and is currently filming the series Really.
