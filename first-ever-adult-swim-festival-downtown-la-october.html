First ever Adult Swim Festival heading to Downtown LA in October

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  202 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

As if we couldn’t get enough of Adult Swim – we do have to thank them for the legendary Rick & Morty – they’ve blessed Southern California with another reason to thank them. Recently it was announced that the first ever Adult Swim Festival will be taking place in Downtown Los Angeles on October 6 & 7.

This festival will feature over 30+ musical acts on two stages, comedic performances, exclusive merch, previews of our favorite shows, and games! If Adult Swim’s presence during San Diego Comic-Con last year is any indication, this festival will be all tons of fun. Just to give you a quick recap – Adult Swim’s presence during SDCC was full of carnival games and free swag for everyone to enjoy.

This year’s lineup includes performances by Big Freedia, Thundercat, Zola Jesus, Run The Jewels, and much more. Check out the teaser for the show below!

Weekend and single day passes are on sale now. Two-day wristbands are $230 + fees and payment plans are available. As for single day, they’re currently going for $75 (20% discount is offered right now!). Parking is sold separately.

Are you thinking about heading out to the Adult Swim Festival? Let us know.
