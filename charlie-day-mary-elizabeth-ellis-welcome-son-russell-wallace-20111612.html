It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia stars Charlie Day and Mary Elizabeth Ellis welcomed a son on Thursday in L.A., her rep confirms to Us Weekly.

This is the first child for Day, 35, and Ellis, 32. The Horrible Bosses star and Ellis wed in March 2006.  Russell Wallace Day weighed in at 7ib. 13 oz. and was 21.5 inches long.

The duo's It's Always Sunny costar Glenn Howerton also became a father for the first time in September. Howerton admitted that their son Miles' moniker was a bit of a last minute decision.
