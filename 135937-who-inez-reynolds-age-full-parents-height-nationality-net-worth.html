Who is Inez Reynolds?

Inez Reynolds is a celebrity child born on 30 September 2016 in New York City, United States. She was born to Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds.

Her mother, Blake Lively, is an American actress, model, and entrepreneur. She was born on 25 August 1987 in Los Angeles, California. Lively gained widespread recognition for her role as Serena van der Woodsen in the television series Gossip Girl.

Her father, Ryan Reynolds, is a Canadian actor, producer, and entrepreneur. He was born on 23 October 1976 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Reynolds began his acting career in the early 1990s and gained recognition for his roles in various television shows, including the sitcom Two Guys and a Girl.

Reynolds first met Blake Lively while co-starring in Green Lantern in early 2010. They started dating in October 2011 and married on September 9, 2012, at Mount Pleasant's Boone Hall Plantation.
