Abe Remarries Paulina, Will His Memories Come Back

 Posted on February 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  469 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Days Of Our Lives spoilers indicate that Paulina Price (Jackee Harry) just may get to marry Abe Carver (James Reynolds) again. Abe doesn’t remember his love for Paulina but he is falling in love with her again. Abe and Paulina could very well decide to get married again before his memories return.

Abe Carver Is Falling In Love Again

Days of Our Lives Spoilers Paulina hasn’t stopped loving Abe, she is just upset that Abe doesn’t remember her. However, Abe has revealed to John Black (Drake Hogestyn) that he is falling for Paulina again and that he is afraid of hurting her.

Abe even considered divorcing Paulina because of this but John got him to change his tune pretty quickly.

Abe may even consider remarriage to prove to Paulina how much he loves her. Abe doesn’t remember their first wedding so why not have a second to declare their love to each other and in front of friends and family?

Will Abe propose to Paulina and make her his wife again? How many times do you get to marry your soulmate twice?

DOOL Spoilers: Paulina Price Is Torn As Well

Paulina confided in Marlena Evans (Deidre Hall) that Abe knew where the decanter was in her apartment. Paulina was sure that it was a memory, but Abe doesn’t necessarily agree.

Paulina feels that she is putting too much pressure on Abe. However, she also wanted Marlena to hypnotize Abe to try and bring back his memory.

Marlena believes they need to slow down and let things develop between them instead of concentrating on the past. Abe may never remember his past but that doesn’t mean he can’t fall for Paulina again.

Can Abe and Paulina not learn to love each other again without the pressure of the past? Will Abe and Paulina remarry now that they have found love again?

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers: Miracles Do Happen

Suppose Abe and Paulina do decide to get remarried and their new ceremony jogs his memory. Abe may subconsciously remember a few things but it would be a miracle if all of his memories were to come flooding back.

Paulina and the rest of the family would be ecstatic if this were to happen since Abe’s memory has been gone for so long.

Abe would be thrilled to remember his kids, his grandchildren, and especially his wife. Abe loves his family and would be thrilled to remember them.

Of course, Nicole Walker (Arianne Zucker) will soon need him as well. Will Abe finally get his memory back and return to his loved ones? Will Abe help Nicole to discover what happened to her baby as well?
