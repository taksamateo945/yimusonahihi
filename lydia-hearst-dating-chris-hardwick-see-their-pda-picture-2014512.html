Lydia Hearst Dating Chris Hardwick: See Their PDA Picture!

 Posted on March 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  216 words  |  Martina Birk

Chris Hardwick has “Singled Out” a new leading lady. The Nerdist podcaster and Talking Dead host is dating model and heiress Lydia Hearst, a source tells Us Weekly, noting that they’ve been together “for a month or so.”

The lovebirds made their big debut as a couple on Thursday, Dec. 4, at the GQ Men of the Year party in L.A., and they weren’t shy about showing their affection. Walking the carpet outside the famed Chateau Marmont, Hardwick, 43, posed with his arm around Hearst, 30, who stunned in a black strapless frock with sheer paneling.

At one point during the photo frenzy, the two even shared a kiss, locking lips in front of a big GQ sign.
