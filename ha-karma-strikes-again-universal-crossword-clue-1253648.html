What are the Types of Crosswords?

Today's crosswords come in a variety of shapes, sizes and levels of difficulty. Throughout the years, creative creators have developed many different types of crosswords. There are two main types of grids, however, there are many variations, such as Sudoku.

The blocked grid format is the most commonly known. This type of crossword consists of a grid with the ends of words and spaces filled in with randomly placed blocked squares. Typically, this format is made up of 15 squares by 15 squares, but it is not mandatory.

On the other hand, barred grids are distinct from blocked grid format with word ends marked by bars. The words are tightly packed and cleverly interwoven, with answers verified by crossing words over each other.
