The Italian side haven't won the European Cup since 1996, losing their last two finals to Borussia Dortmund and Real Madrid. They go in search of their third title, having been runners-up five times.

Barca, on the other hand, have won the competition four times. Three of those triumphs have come since 2006, with the most recent being 2013. The Catalan club have lost three finals.

Both sides have won their domestic league and cup this season and are looking to confirm a historic treble in Germany.
