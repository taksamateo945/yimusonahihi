Bristol Palin Is 7 Months Pregnant and &quot;Having the Best Time&quot;: Photo

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  275 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Pregnant and happy! Bristol Palin shared a photo on Instagram on Monday, Oct. 5, with her son, Tripp, and another young boy, updating her followers on her pregnancy.

“#7monthspregnant but still the best time with my boys,” Sarah Palin’s daughter, 24, wrote. In the photo, Bristol looks happy, rocking shades and a black top. She’s accompanied by her 6-year-old son Tripp with ex fiance Levi Johnston, and a boy who appears to be her youngest brother, at what looks like a resort or pool, all wearing swimming gear.

The former Dancing With the Stars contestant is expecting her second child. She confirmed the pregnancy in a blog post on June 25, referring to her pregnancy as a “disappointment.”

“I know this has been, and will be, a huge disappointment to my family, to my close friends, and to many of you,” she wrote. “But please respect Tripp’s and my privacy during this time. I do not want any lectures and I do not want any sympathy.”

The reality personality quickly defended the pregnancy after receiving backlash a few days later on June 28.
